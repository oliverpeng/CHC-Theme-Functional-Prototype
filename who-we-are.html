Who We Are

Churches Helping Churches is a global fellowship of church communities who seek to rebuild other churches in the wake of a catastrophic natural disaster. Importantly, we are not Churches Helping People or even Christians Helping Churches. Rather, we are church communities from all over the world who have partnered through CHC with other church communities of brothers and sisters who are suffering.

History

CHC was founded by Pastor James MacDonald of Harvest Bible Chapel and Pastor Mark Driscoll of Mars Hill Church following the 7.0-magnitude earthquake that hit Port-au-Prince, Haiti on January 12. Read the full story.
